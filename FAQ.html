What is Alesta Dynamics?

AlestA Dynamics is an integrated engineering portfolio comprising a collection of projects, documents, and resources developed by Ege. It focuses on autonomous systems and modular hardware design.

Why the name "Alesta"?

In maritime terminology, "Alesta" means "ready" or "on standby." It represents the system's readiness for rapid deployment and tactical response.
